Qore Programming Language

Embeddable Multithreaded Programming Language

The Qore programming language is a multithreaded interpreted scripting language with support for strong typing, prompt collection, procedural, object-oriented, and functional features as well as sandboxed application scripting (Qore embedded in Qore or C++).

Qore is free to use, even for commercial closed-source projects due to its licensing policy (choice of MIT, LGPL v2.1 or GPL licenses).

Qore runs on Linux/Unix, macOS, and Windows.
